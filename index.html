Serving the Community Since 'Yesterday'

Local Man Single-Handedly Wards Off Wild Hog

In a surprising turn of events on Tuesday afternoon, local resident Barry "The Basher" Henderson encountered a rogue wild hog near his property. Eyewitness reports suggest a brief but intense standoff, culminating in Mr. Henderson successfully deterring the animal. "He wanted it to be one way... but it's the other way," commented a shaken but unharmed Mr. Henderson.

By Our Staff Reporter

Community Garden Harvest Festival a Resounding Success

The annual Community Garden Harvest Festival took place this past weekend, drawing large crowds and celebrating the fruits (and vegetables) of local labor. Highlights included a prize-winning pumpkin contest, live music, and a bake sale featuring goods made with garden-fresh ingredients. Organizers hailed the event as a resounding success, fostering community spirit and promoting sustainable living.

Upcoming Town Hall Meeting Scheduled

Residents of Pasadena are invited to attend a town hall meeting next Thursday at 7 PM in the community center. The agenda includes discussions on local infrastructure improvements and upcoming zoning changes. This is an opportunity for citizens to voice their opinions and engage with local officials.

Local Sports Team Wins Championship

The Pasadena Panthers have clinched the regional championship title after a hard-fought season. Their final game against rival Northwood High saw a thrilling finish, with the Panthers securing victory in the last minutes. The team will be celebrating their win with a parade downtown next Saturday.
